Frequently Asked Questions About Happy Hour Specials

What are SESAMO's happy hour specials and when do they run?
At SESAMO in Hell's Kitchen, our happy hour runs Monday through Friday from 4:00 PM to 7:00 PM. Specials include select Italian wines and classic cocktails for $8, draft beers for $6, and a rotating menu of small plate appetizers like arancini and bruschetta for $7 each. We update our happy hour menu seasonally, so feel free to ask your server for the latest offerings.
Do I need to make a reservation for happy hour, or can I just walk in?
While walk-ins are always welcome at SESAMO, happy hour tables can fill up quickly, especially after work hours. We recommend making a reservation for groups of four or more to ensure seating, particularly if you want to enjoy happy hour on our outdoor patio or prefer a specific area. You can reserve a table online or by calling us directly.
Are there any restrictions on happy hour specials due to New York City licensing laws?
Yes, as per New York State liquor laws, happy hour specials on alcoholic beverages are only available for consumption on-premises and during designated hours (4:00 PM to 7:00 PM). We cannot extend happy hour pricing outside these times or offer 'bottomless' drink deals. All guests enjoying alcoholic beverages must be 21 or older with valid ID.
Can I combine happy hour specials with other promotions or discounts?
Happy hour pricing at SESAMO cannot be combined with other promotions, discounts, or prix fixe menus. Each offer is separate to ensure we can provide the best value during our designated happy hour period. If you have any questions about specific promotions, our staff will be happy to clarify before you order.
What is the process for booking a group happy hour event or private party at SESAMO?
To book a group happy hour or private event at SESAMO, please contact our events coordinator at least one week in advance. We offer semi-private and private spaces for groups, with customizable appetizer platters and drink packages. A deposit may be required to secure your date. We'll work with you to finalize the menu, timeline, and any special requests to ensure your event runs smoothly. Reach out by phone or through our website's event inquiry form for details.
