Frequently Asked Questions About Chicken Parm

Please let your server know about any dietary preferences, and we’ll do our best to accommodate you.
Do I need to make a reservation for dinner, or can I walk in for Chicken Parm?
While we always welcome walk-ins at SESAMO, evenings—especially Friday and Saturday—can get busy in Hell's Kitchen.
