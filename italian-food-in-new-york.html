Yes, SESAMO welcomes reservations and highly recommends them, especially for dinner and weekend seatings, as Hell's Kitchen can get busy. At SESAMO, appetizers generally range from $12–$20, and main courses are typically $22–$34. We also offer a three-course prix fixe menu for $48 per person, which is popular before Broadway shows. Wine and cocktails are additional. Tax and gratuity are not included in menu prices.

Yes, SESAMO is fully licensed to serve beer, wine, and spirits in New York. We feature a curated Italian wine list, including by-the-glass and bottle options, as well as classic and house-specialty cocktails. Our staff is happy to recommend pairings for your meal.

A typical dinner at SESAMO lasts about 90 minutes. For guests attending Broadway shows in the area, let us know your showtime when booking—our team is experienced in providing prompt service to ensure you finish your meal comfortably before curtain time.
