Frequently Asked Questions About Private Dining Events

What is the process for booking a private dining event at SESAMO in Hell's Kitchen?
To book a private dining event at SESAMO, start by contacting our events manager via our website or by phone. We’ll discuss your group size, preferred date, menu options, and any special requests. Once details are confirmed, we’ll send a customized proposal. A deposit is required to secure your date, and final guest count and menu selections are due one week prior to your event. Our team will guide you through each step to ensure a seamless experience.
How much does it cost to host a private event at SESAMO, and are there minimum spend requirements?
Our private dining pricing varies based on the day, time, group size, and menu selections. In general, there is a food and beverage minimum spend, which starts at $1,500 for smaller groups on weekdays and may be higher for weekends or full buyouts. We offer prix fixe family-style Italian menus with vegetarian and gluten-free options. Please contact us with your event details for a detailed quote tailored to your needs.
Can SESAMO accommodate special dietary needs or allergies for private dining events?
Absolutely. We pride ourselves on accommodating dietary preferences and allergies, including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and nut-free options. Please let us know about any dietary restrictions when booking, and our chef will work with you to ensure all guests are taken care of. We’re happy to provide allergen information for all menu items upon request.
Is SESAMO licensed to serve alcohol, and can we bring our own wine or spirits for our private event?
SESAMO is fully licensed to serve beer, wine, and spirits in accordance with New York State law. We offer curated beverage packages and can create custom cocktails for your event. Outside alcohol is not permitted, with the exception of wine, for which a corkage fee of $35 per 750ml bottle applies. Please inform us in advance if you plan to bring your own wine.
What happens if we need to cancel or reschedule our private dining event?
We understand that plans can change. If you need to cancel, please notify us as soon as possible. Deposits are refundable up to 14 days before the event date; cancellations within 14 days will forfeit the deposit. If you need to reschedule, we’ll do our best to accommodate your new date and transfer your deposit, subject to availability. Please review our full cancellation policy when booking.
