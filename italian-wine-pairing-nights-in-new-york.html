Frequently Asked Questions About Italian Wine Pairing Nights

How much does it cost to attend an Italian wine pairing night at SESAMO in Hell's Kitchen?
Our Italian wine pairing nights typically range from $75 to $110 per person, depending on the featured menu and wines for the evening. This price includes a multi-course Italian meal, expertly paired wines, and gratuity. We recommend checking our events calendar on the SESAMO website or calling us directly, as pricing may vary for special guest winemakers or exclusive vintages.
How far in advance should I book a spot for a wine pairing night, and how do I reserve?
Wine pairing nights at SESAMO are popular and tend to sell out quickly, especially in the Hell's Kitchen area. We suggest booking at least two weeks in advance. Reservations can be made directly through our website, by phone, or in person at the restaurant. We require a credit card to hold your spot, and will send a confirmation email with event details.
Is SESAMO licensed to serve alcohol and offer wine pairings in New York City?
Yes, SESAMO is fully licensed by the New York State Liquor Authority to serve wine, beer, and spirits on our premises. Our Italian wine pairing nights are curated by our in-house sommelier and team, ensuring all legal requirements and quality standards are met.
What can I expect during an Italian wine pairing night at SESAMO?
You'll enjoy a chef-crafted multi-course Italian menu, with each dish thoughtfully paired with select Italian wines. Our sommelier will introduce each wine, share background stories about the wineries, and offer tasting notes. The evening typically lasts about two and a half hours, and there's time for Q&A with our chef and wine experts. It's a relaxed, interactive experience designed for everyone from wine novices to enthusiasts.
What happens if I need to cancel my reservation, or if the event is rescheduled?
We understand plans can change. If you need to cancel your reservation, please let us know at least 48 hours in advance for a full refund. Cancellations within 48 hours may be subject to a partial charge, as ingredients and wines are ordered specifically for the event. If SESAMO needs to reschedule or cancel a wine pairing night (such as due to weather or vendor issues), you will receive a full refund or the option to transfer your reservation to a future event.
